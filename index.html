Quickstart — first 10 minutes

1) Get a token

curl -X POST https://sandbox.api.yourapi.com/oauth/token \
  -d 'client_id=...' -d 'client_secret=...'

2) Make your first call

curl -H "Authorization: Bearer <token>" \
  https://sandbox.api.yourapi.com/ping

3) See a working response

HTTP/1.1 200 OK
{"status":"ok"}

4) If it fails

HTTP/1.1 401 Unauthorized
{"type":"about:blank","title":"Invalid token"}
